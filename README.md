Master's in Sound and Music Computing - DSP for Sound and Music (First Trimester)

This repository contains course assignments and lab exercises from the "DSP for Sound and Music" course, 
part of the Master's program in Sound and Music Computing at Universitat Pompeu Fabra. All exercises are based on the SMS-Tools 
educational materials and cover fundamental digital signal processing techniques for audio analysis and synthesis.

Topics covered include Fourier analysis, STFT, sinusoidal modeling, harmonic analysis, and sound transformations.
